<p><p>The Remote Telecontrol Units (RTUs) market comprises two primary types: wireless RTUs and wired RTUs. Wireless RTUs utilize radio frequencies, cellular networks, or satellite communication for data transmission, offering flexibility and easier installation in remote locations. In contrast, wired RTUs connect directly to control systems using cables, ensuring stable and secure communication with minimal latency, typically suited for urban or industrial environments. Both types play crucial roles in monitoring and controlling systems across various sectors including utilities, oil and gas, and transportation.</p></p>
