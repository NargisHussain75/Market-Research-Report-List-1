<p><p>Ventricular Assistance Devices (VADs) are critical for supporting heart function in patients with severe heart failure. The Right Ventricular Assist Device (RVAD) supports the right ventricle, aiding blood flow to the lungs. The Left Ventricular Assist Device (LVAD) assists the left ventricle, enhancing blood circulation to the body. Bi-ventricular Assist Devices (BiVADs) support both ventricles, offering comprehensive assistance in advanced heart failure cases. These devices are essential in managing heart conditions and improving patient outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
