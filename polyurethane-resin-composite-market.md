<p><strong>The Polyurethane Resin Composite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pultrusion</li><li>Winding</li><li>Perfusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane resin composite market encompasses three primary manufacturing processes: pultrusion, winding, and perfusion. </p><p>Pultrusion involves pulling reinforced fibers through a resin bath and heating them, creating continuous composite structures ideal for consistent cross-sections. Winding uses a rotating mandrel to layer resin-impregnated fibers, producing cylindrical parts with high strength and lightweight properties. Perfusion involves the infusion of resin into a fibrous preform, optimizing fiber-to-resin ratios and enabling complex shapes, making it suitable for larger components with enhanced performance characteristics.</p></p>
