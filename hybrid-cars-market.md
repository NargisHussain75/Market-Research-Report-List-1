<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Hybrid Cars Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Series Hybrid</li><li>Parallel Hybrid</li><li>Plug-in Hybrid</li><li>Series-Parallel Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Hybrid cars are categorized into four main market types based on their powertrain configurations. </p><p>1. **Series Hybrid**: The electric motor drives the wheels, while the internal combustion engine generates electricity to power the motor or charge the battery. </p><p>   </p><p>2. **Parallel Hybrid**: Both the internal combustion engine and electric motor can power the vehicle directly.</p><p>3. **Plug-in Hybrid**: Similar to parallel hybrids but with larger batteries, allowing external charging for extended electric-only driving.</p><p>4. **Series-Parallel Hybrid**: Combines both configurations, optimizing efficiency by allowing the vehicle to switch between electric and gasoline power as needed.</p></p>
