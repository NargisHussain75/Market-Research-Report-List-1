<p><p>The height-adjustable shower trolley market comprises three main types: manual, electric, and hydraulic trolleys. Manual shower trolleys require physical effort to adjust height, making them cost-effective but labor-intensive. Electric shower trolleys offer automated adjustments with the push of a button, enhancing ease of use, especially for caregivers. Hydraulic shower trolleys utilize hydraulic systems for smooth height adjustment, providing a balance between manual and electric options, and are valued for their stability and reliability in various healthcare settings.</p></p>
