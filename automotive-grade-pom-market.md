<p><p>The automotive grade POM (Polyoxymethylene) market is divided into two main types: homopolymer POM and copolymer POM. Homopolymer POM is known for its high stiffness, strength, and excellent dimensional stability, making it ideal for precision automotive components. In contrast, copolymer POM offers enhanced toughness and chemical resistance, which is beneficial for applications requiring more durability under varying conditions. Both types are crucial for manufacturing lightweight, high-performance parts that improve fuel efficiency and reduce emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
