<p><p>The solar photovoltaics wafer market is primarily segmented into two types: monocrystalline wafers and multicrystalline (or polycrystalline) wafers. Monocrystalline wafers, made from a single crystal structure, offer higher efficiency and space-saving designs, making them ideal for residential and commercial applications. Multicrystalline wafers, composed of multiple crystal structures, are generally less efficient but more cost-effective, making them popular for large-scale projects. Both types play a crucial role in the growing renewable energy sector, catering to diverse consumer needs and budget considerations.</p></p>
