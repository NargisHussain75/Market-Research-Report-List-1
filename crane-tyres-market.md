<p><strong>The Crane Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bias Ply</li><li>Radial Ply</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The crane tyre market primarily consists of two types: bias ply and radial ply tyres. Bias ply tyres feature layers of fabric that crisscross at angles, providing durability and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for rough terrains. Radial ply tyres, on the other hand, have layers arranged radially, offering improved flexibility, better fuel efficiency, and superior traction. This differentiation influences performance characteristics, longevity, and cost, catering to specific operational needs in various crane applications. This segmentation helps meet diverse demands in the construction and lifting industries.</p></p>
