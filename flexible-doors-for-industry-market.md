<p><p>Flexible doors for the industrial sector are categorized into three main types: fixed, hinged, and sliding. Fixed doors provide a permanent barrier, ideal for maintaining temperature or preventing contamination. Hinged doors swing open on a pivot, allowing easy access while saving space, suitable for high-traffic areas. Sliding doors operate parallel to the wall, maximizing opening space and efficiency, perfect for large openings or tight spaces. Each type serves specific operational needs while enhancing workflow and safety in industrial environments.</p></p>
