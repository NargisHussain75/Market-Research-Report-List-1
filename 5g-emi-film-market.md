<p><p>The 5G EMI film market consists of various types, including metal-mesh film and coating film. Metal-mesh films utilize a network of conductive metals that provide excellent electromagnetic interference (EMI) shielding while maintaining transparency, making them suitable for screens and windows. Coating films, on the other hand, involve applying a conductive layer to substrates, offering flexibility and ease of application. Both types play a vital role in enhancing the performance and reliability of 5G technology by ensuring signal integrity and reducing interference.</p></p>
