<p><p>The Prostate Specific Antigen (PSA) testing market is primarily divided into two types: Radioimmunoassay (RIA) and Enzyme Immunoassay (EIA). RIA utilizes radioactive isotopes to detect PSA levels with high sensitivity and specificity but is limited by safety concerns. In contrast, EIA employs enzyme-linked reactions, offering faster results and easier handling, making it more popular in clinical settings. Both methods are essential for diagnosing and monitoring prostate health, influencing treatment decisions and patient outcomes.</p></p>
