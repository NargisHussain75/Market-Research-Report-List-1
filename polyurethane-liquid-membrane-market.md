<p><strong>The Polyurethane Liquid Membrane Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane</li><li>Two-component Polyurethane Liquid Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane liquid membrane market is categorized into two main types: one-component and two-component membranes. One-component polyurethane liquid membranes are ready-to-use and cure through moisture in the air, offering ease of application and quick drying. In contrast, two-component polyurethane liquid membranes require the mixing of a resin and hardener before application, providing enhanced durability and chemical resistance. Both types are widely used in waterproofing, roofing, and industrial applications due to their strong protective properties.</p></p>
