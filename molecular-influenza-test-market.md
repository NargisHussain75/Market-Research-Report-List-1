<p><p>The molecular influenza test market includes several types of diagnostic technologies. RT-PCR (Reverse Transcription Polymerase Chain Reaction) is highly sensitive and widely used for detecting influenza viruses. TMA (Transcription-Mediated Amplification) offers rapid results through an amplification process. LAMP (Loop-Mediated Isothermal Amplification) provides a simple, quick alternative for point-of-care testing. NASBA (Nucleic Acid Sequence-Based Amplification) is another method suitable for detecting RNA viruses. Other market types encompass various innovative technologies aimed at improving influenza diagnosis.</p></p>
