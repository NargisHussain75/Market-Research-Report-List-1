<p><strong>The Automobile Rear Axle Assembly Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Full-Floating Type</li><li>Semi-Floating Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile rear axle assembly market is primarily categorized into two types: full-floating and semi-floating axles. Full-floating axles feature a design where the axle shaft is completely supported by bearings, allowing the vehicle's weight to bear on the axle housing. This design offers enhanced load capacity and durability, making it ideal for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, semi-floating axles have the axle shaft supported by both the wheel and the axle housing, providing a balance of strength and cost-effectiveness for lighter vehicles.</p></p>
