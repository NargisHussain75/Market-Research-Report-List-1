<p><strong>The IV Connector Without Needle Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Fluid Displacement</li><li>Negative Fluid Displacement</li><li>Neutral Displacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IV connector without needle market is categorized into three types based on fluid displacement: </p><p>1. **Positive Fluid Displacement**: This type delivers fluid effectively, ensuring that the entire volume is pushed into the vein, minimizing residual fluid in the line.</p><p>2. **Negative Fluid Displacement**: In this design, fluid is expelled out of the connector, leading to less fluid in the line after use, which can reduce the risk of air embolism.</p><p>3. **Neutral Displacement**: This maintains the original fluid volume, minimizing blood reflux and ensuring more consistent medication delivery.</p></p>
