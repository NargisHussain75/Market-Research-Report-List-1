<p><p>The automotive sound system market is divided into OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) and aftermarket segments. OEM systems are factory-installed by vehicle manufacturers, offering seamless integration and reliability but often limited customization. Aftermarket systems are purchased separately and installed post-purchase, providing enhanced performance, advanced features, and greater personalization. While OEM systems cater to standard preferences, aftermarket solutions appeal to enthusiasts seeking superior audio quality and tailored upgrades. Both markets coexist, addressing diverse consumer needs for in-car audio experiences.</p></p>
