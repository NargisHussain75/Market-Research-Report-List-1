<p><strong>The Construction Laser Level Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Line Laser Level</li><li>Rotation Laser Level</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The construction laser level market includes various types, primarily line laser levels and rotation laser levels. Line laser levels project a straight line horizontally or vertically, making them ideal for tasks like aligning shelves or laying tiles. In contrast, rotation laser levels emit a 360-degree rotating beam, providing a reference plane over larger distances, which is essential for grading, leveling, and site layout in construction projects. Both types enhance accuracy and efficiency in construction tasks.</p></p>
