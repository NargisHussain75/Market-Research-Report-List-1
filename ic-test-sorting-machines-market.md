<p><strong>The IC Test Sorting Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pick-And-Place Handlers</li><li>Gravity-Feed Handlers</li><li>Turret Handlers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IC Test Sorting Machines are essential for efficiently managing integrated circuit testing processes. The market includes three primary types: </p><p>1. **Pick-And-Place Handlers**: These machines automate the movement of ICs, picking them from testing stations and placing them into packaging or storage, enhancing speed and precision. </p><p>2. **Gravity-Feed Handlers**: Utilizing gravity to move ICs through testing sequences, they are cost-effective and simple, ideal for high-volume operations. </p><p>3. **Turret Handlers**: Featuring a rotating mechanism, these handlers facilitate rapid switching between different test processes, accommodating multiple IC types efficiently.</p></p>
