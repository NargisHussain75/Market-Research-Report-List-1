<p><strong>The PPS Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Linear Type</li><li>Cross-linked Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PPS resin market is categorized into Linear Type and Cross-linked Type based on molecular structure. Linear Type PPS resins have a straight-chain structure, offering excellent mechanical strength, thermal stability, and chemical resistance, making them ideal for automotive and electronics applications. Cross-linked Type PPS resins feature a three-dimensional network, enhancing heat resistance and dimensional stability, suitable for high-temperature environments like aerospace and industrial components. Both types cater to diverse industries, balancing performance and application-specific requirements.</p></p>
