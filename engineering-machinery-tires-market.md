<p><ul><li>Bias Tire</li><li>Radial Tire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Engineering Machinery Tires market primarily includes Bias and Radial tires. Bias tires, constructed with crisscrossed ply layers, offer robust durability and load-bearing capacity, ideal for heavy-duty applications like construction and mining. Radial tires, with perpendicular ply layers, provide better fuel efficiency, longer tread life, and improved traction, making them suitable for high-speed operations and smoother terrains. While bias tires excel in rugged environments, radial tires are preferred for efficiency and performance, catering to diverse industrial needs. Both types serve distinct roles in machinery operations.</p></p>
