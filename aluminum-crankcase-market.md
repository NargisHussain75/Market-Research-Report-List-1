<p><ul><li>Upper Crankcase</li><li>Lower Crankcase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aluminum crankcase market is divided into two primary types: upper crankcase and lower crankcase. The upper crankcase typically houses critical engine components like the camshaft and valve assemblies, providing structural support and housing for moving parts. In contrast, the lower crankcase serves as the oil reservoir and supports the crankshaft, vital for engine lubrication and performance. Both types are essential for engine efficiency, durability, and weight reduction, driving the demand for aluminum crankcases in automotive and industrial applications.</p></p>
