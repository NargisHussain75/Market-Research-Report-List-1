<p><p>The Car Hub Bearing market is categorized into Gen. 1, Gen. 2, and Gen. 3 bearings based on design and functionality. Gen. 1 bearings are traditional, consisting of separate components like ball bearings and hubs. Gen. 2 bearings integrate the hub and bearing into a single unit, improving durability and reducing weight. Gen. 3 bearings further enhance performance by incorporating advanced materials and designs, offering better load capacity, efficiency, and integration with vehicle systems, catering to modern automotive demands.</p></p>
