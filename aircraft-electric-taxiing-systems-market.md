<p><p>The Aircraft Electric Taxiing Systems (AETS) market is categorized into non-vehicular and vehicular aircraft Electric Ground Taxiing Systems (EGTS). Non-vehicular aircraft EGTS integrates electric motors into the aircraft's landing gear, allowing independent ground movement without engines. In contrast, vehicular aircraft EGTS utilizes ground vehicles, such as tugs or electric carts, to maneuver aircraft on the taxiway. Both systems aim to enhance fuel efficiency, reduce emissions, and lower operational costs, transforming traditional aircraft ground handling practices.</p></p>
