<p><p>The solar energy products market is primarily categorized into three types of solar cells. Monocrystalline solar cells, known for their high efficiency and space-saving design, are made from single-crystal silicon. Polycrystalline silicon solar cells are composed of multiple silicon crystals, offering a cost-effective solution with slightly lower efficiency. Amorphous silicon solar cells are non-crystalline and provide flexibility and lightweight options, suitable for niche applications. Each type caters to different consumer needs and balances efficiency, cost, and application versatility.</p></p>
