<p><p>Ultrafast rectifier diodes are essential in power conversion applications due to their rapid switching capabilities and low forward voltage drop. The market is segmented into two main types: Single Common Anode and Dual Common Anode. Single Common Anode diodes use one common anode terminal for multiple cathodes, making them suitable for simpler circuits. In contrast, Dual Common Anode diodes have two distinct anodes, allowing for increased flexibility and efficiency in more complex applications, such as power supplies and inverters.</p></p>
