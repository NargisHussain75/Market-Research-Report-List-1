<p><ul><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Fully Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Self-checkout machines are classified into two primary market types: semi-automatic and fully automatic. Semi-automatic machines require some manual intervention from customers, such as scanning items and bagging, while still streamlining the checkout process. Fully automatic machines, on the other hand, handle nearly all aspects of the transaction independently, including scanning, weighing, and payment processing. Both types enhance customer convenience and reduce labor costs for retailers, catering to varying consumer preferences and operational needs.</p></p>
