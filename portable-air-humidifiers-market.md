<p><p>The portable air humidifiers market is primarily categorized into warm mist and cool mist humidifiers. Warm mist humidifiers use a heating element to boil water, releasing warm steam, which can help alleviate respiratory issues and provide soothing comfort in colder environments. In contrast, cool mist humidifiers release a fine mist of water without heating, making them ideal for warmer climates and safer for use around children. Both types are designed to maintain optimal humidity levels, enhancing indoor air quality and overall comfort.</p></p>
