<p><p>The non-linear resistors market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **VDR Resistor (Voltage Dependent Resistor)** - These resistors vary resistance based on voltage changes, providing over-voltage protection.  </p><p>   </p><p>2. **PTC Resistor (Positive Temperature Coefficient)** - Their resistance increases with temperature, used mainly in overcurrent protection applications.  </p><p>3. **NTC Resistor (Negative Temperature Coefficient)** - These exhibit decreased resistance with rising temperature, commonly utilized in temperature sensing and circuit protection.  </p><p>4. **LDR Resistor (Light Dependent Resistor)** - Their resistance decreases with increasing light intensity, widely used in light-sensing applications.</p></p>
