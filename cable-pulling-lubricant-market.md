<p><p>The cable pulling lubricant market is primarily categorized into two types: liquid and gel. Liquid lubricants are commonly used for large-scale installations, as they provide excellent flow and reduce friction effectively, making cable installation smoother. On the other hand, gel lubricants offer a thicker consistency, adhering better to cables and reducing slippage during installation. This type is especially useful in vertical or overhead applications. Both types are essential for enhancing the efficiency and safety of cable pulling operations.</p></p>
