<p><p>The excavator OTR tire market primarily consists of two types: radial tires and bias tires. Radial tires have a construction where the tire plies are arranged at 90 degrees to the direction of travel, providing advantages such as improved fuel efficiency, better traction, and enhanced comfort. Bias tires, on the other hand, feature plies that crisscross, offering durability and resistance to punctures, making them suitable for rugged terrains. The choice between these two types often depends on the specific application and operational conditions.</p></p>
