<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Touch Switch market is categorized into Type I and Type II. Type I refers to capacitive touch switches, which detect changes in capacitance caused by finger touch, offering high sensitivity and sleek designs. Type II consists of resistive touch switches, which respond to pressure on the surface, being more durable and suitable for various environmental conditions. Each type serves distinct applications in automotive interiors, enhancing user interaction and functionality in vehicle control systems.</p></p>
