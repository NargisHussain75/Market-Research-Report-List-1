<p><ul><li>SBS Type</li><li>APP Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Modified Bitumen Roofing Membranes are primarily categorized into two types: SBS (Styrene-Butadiene-Styrene) and APP (Atactic Polypropylene). SBS membranes offer enhanced elasticity and flexibility, making them suitable for regions with temperature fluctuations. Conversely, APP membranes provide superior UV resistance and are more resistant to aging, ideal for hot climates. Both types are applied using torch-on, cold adhesive, or self-adhered methods, catering to diverse roofing needs while ensuring durability, weather resistance, and ease of installation across various applications.</p></p>
