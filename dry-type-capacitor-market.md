<p><p>The dry-type capacitor market comprises three main types: solid filling, semi-solid filling, and gas filling. Solid filling capacitors use a porous dielectric material, providing excellent thermal and electrical performance. Semi-solid filling capacitors utilize a combination of solid and liquid materials, balancing performance and cost. Gas filling capacitors employ gases like nitrogen to enhance efficiency and heat dissipation. Each type caters to specific application needs, such as power electronics and renewable energy systems, driven by demands for reliability and performance.</p></p>
