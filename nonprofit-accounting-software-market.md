<p><p>Nonprofit accounting software is primarily offered in two market types: cloud-based and premise-based. Cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessible via the internet, offering flexibility, automatic updates, and scalability, making it ideal for organizations with varying needs. Conversely, premise-based software is installed on local servers, providing organizations with greater control over data and security but requiring upfront hardware investments and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to distinct management styles and operational requirements of nonprofits.</p></p>
