<p><p>The Glycol Ether market is primarily categorized into two types: E-Series and P-Series. E-Series Glycol Ethers, derived from ethylene oxide, are known for their solvent properties and are commonly used in paints, coatings, and cleaning products due to their excellent evaporation rates and low toxicity. P-Series Glycol Ethers, derived from propylene oxide, offer superior performance in applications requiring higher solvency and stability, making them ideal for industrial cleaning, cosmetics, and personal care formulations. Both categories serve distinct market needs.</p></p>
