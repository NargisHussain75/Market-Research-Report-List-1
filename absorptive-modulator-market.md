<p><p>The absorptive modulator market comprises both passive and active components. Passive components include resistive devices that absorb excess signals without amplification, helping in signal management and noise reduction. Active components, on the other hand, involve amplifiers or other powered devices that actively modify signal characteristics, enhancing performance and functionality. Together, these components enable efficient signal modulation in various applications, including telecommunications and broadcasting, by balancing signal strength and quality while mitigating interference.</p></p>
