<p><p>pH measuring devices are essential tools used to determine the acidity or alkalinity of a solution. The market is primarily divided into two types: pointer and digital display devices. Pointer pH meters utilize analog displays with a needle indicator, providing a quick visual reading but requiring careful interpretation.
