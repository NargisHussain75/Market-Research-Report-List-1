<p><p>The light vehicle starter and alternator market comprises two main components: the alternator and the starter motor. Alternators generate electrical power to charge the vehicle's battery and supply energy to the electrical systems when the engine is running. Starter motors, on the other hand, initiate the engine's operation by turning it over. Both components are crucial for the efficient functioning of light vehicles, ensuring reliable starting and consistent electrical supply, ultimately influencing overall vehicle performance and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
