<p><ul><li>Door Station</li><li>Video Intercom Master</li><li>Indoor Units</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Video door entry devices enhance security and convenience in residential and commercial properties. The market is segmented into three main types: </p><p>1. **Door Station**: This exterior unit houses the camera and microphone, allowing users to see and communicate with visitors. </p><p>2. **Video Intercom Master**: Positioned within a property, this central unit receives feeds from multiple door stations, enabling users to manage access and monitor entry points. </p><p>3. **Indoor Units**: These are users' terminal devices for viewing and interacting with visitors, offering features like touchscreens and call-waiting functionalities. Together, they create a cohesive video intercom system for enhanced access control.</p></p>
