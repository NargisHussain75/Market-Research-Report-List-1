<p><ul><li>Crawler Type</li><li>Wheel Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Asphalt Transfer Vehicles (ATVs) are classified into two primary types: crawler type and wheel type. Crawler type ATVs utilize tracks for improved stability and traction on uneven surfaces, making them ideal for rugged terrain and large-scale projects. In contrast, wheel type ATVs are equipped with wheels, offering greater maneuverability and speed on flat surfaces. Both types enhance efficiency in transporting asphalt from storage to paving machines, but their selection depends on specific project requirements and site conditions.</p></p>
