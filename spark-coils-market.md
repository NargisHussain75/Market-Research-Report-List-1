<p><strong>The Spark Coils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-spark</li><li>Multi-spark</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Spark Coils market is categorized into two primary types: single-spark and multi-spark. Single-spark coils generate one spark per ignition cycle, commonly used in standard internal combustion engines for efficient combustion. In contrast, multi-spark coils produce multiple sparks during each cycle, enhancing fuel atomization and combustion efficiency, making them popular in performance and racing applications. Each type serves distinct automotive needs, with single-spark coils focusing on reliability and multi-spark coils aiming to improve engine performance and responsiveness.</p></p>
