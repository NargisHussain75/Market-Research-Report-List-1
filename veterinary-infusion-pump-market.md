<p><p>The veterinary infusion pump market comprises primarily two types: large-volume infusion pumps and syringe infusion pumps. Large-volume infusion pumps are designed to deliver large amounts of fluids, medications, or nutrients over extended periods, making them ideal for critical care situations in animals. Syringe infusion pumps, on the other hand, provide precise control over smaller volumes of fluid, allowing for accurate dosing in smaller patients or for specific medications. Both types enhance treatment efficacy and safety in veterinary practices.</p></p>
