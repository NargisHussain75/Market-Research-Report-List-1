<p><ul><li>Impregnated Paper Cable</li><li>Self-Contained Oil-Filled Cable</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The submarine cables market predominantly features two types: impregnated paper cables and self-contained oil-filled cables. Impregnated paper cables utilize layers of insulating paper soaked in oil, providing moisture resistance and stability, making them ideal for deeper installations. In contrast, self-contained oil-filled cables have an inner oil-filled core, offering enhanced thermal performance and flexibility for various depths and conditions. Both types are crucial for transmitting electricity and communication signals across oceans, supporting global connectivity and energy distribution.</p></p>
