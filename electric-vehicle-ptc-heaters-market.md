<p><p>The electric vehicle PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) heaters market comprises three primary types: immersion heaters, radiator heaters, and pad style heaters. Immersion heaters are designed to heat fluids directly, efficiently warming the vehicle's coolant. Radiator heaters distribute heat through the cabin, providing consistent warmth to occupants. Pad style heaters are installed beneath surfaces to provide localized heating, enhancing comfort and preventing cold spots. Each type serves distinct functions, contributing to the overall thermal management and comfort in electric vehicles.</p></p>
