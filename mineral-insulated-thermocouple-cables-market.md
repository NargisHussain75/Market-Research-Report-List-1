<p><p>The mineral insulated thermocouple cables market primarily includes two types: simplex and duplex configurations. Two conductors (simplex) consist of a single pair of thermoelement wires, suitable for basic temperature measurement applications. Four conductors (duplex) feature two pairs of wires, allowing for redundant temperature readings or differential measurements, enhancing accuracy and reliability. Other variations may include specialized configurations tailored for particular industrial applications, such as high-temperature or corrosive environments, maximizing performance and durability in challenging conditions.</p></p>
