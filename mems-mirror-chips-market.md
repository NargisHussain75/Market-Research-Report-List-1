<p><strong>The MEMS Mirror Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1-axis Scan Mirror</li><li>2-axis Scan Mirror</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The MEMS mirror chips market is divided into two primary types: 1-axis scan mirrors and 2-axis scan mirrors. 1-axis scan mirrors enable oscillation around a single axis, primarily used in applications like laser beam steering and optical communications. Conversely, 2-axis scan mirrors can tilt around both horizontal and vertical axes, allowing for more complex motion and greater versatility, making them ideal for applications such as 3D imaging and LiDAR systems. Both types are essential for advancing optical technologies.</p></p>
