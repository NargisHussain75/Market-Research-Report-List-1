<p><p>The solid carbide milling cutter market includes various types, primarily categorized into straight shank, taper shank, and others. Straight shank cutters have a uniform diameter along their length, making them versatile and easy to use with standard tool holders. Taper shank cutters have a gradually reducing diameter, enabling better grip and stability in high-speed applications. The "others" category encompasses specialized designs, such as ball nose or corner radius cutters, catering to specific machining needs and materials.</p></p>
