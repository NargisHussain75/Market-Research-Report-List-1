<p><p>The Laser-Beam Welding Consumables market includes various types that enhance welding efficiency. Stick electrodes are solid rods used for manual arc welding, while solid wires provide continuous feeding for MIG welding. Flux-cored wires contain a core of flux to improve welding in challenging conditions. Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) wires are used in automated processes with added flux for protection. Fluxes are materials that prevent oxidation and improve weld quality. These consumables play a critical role in achieving strong, precise welds across industries.</p></p>
