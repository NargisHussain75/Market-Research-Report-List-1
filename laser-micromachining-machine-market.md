<p><p>The laser micromachining machine market is divided into two primary types: UV lasers and green lasers. UV lasers, known for their high precision and ability to create fine features, are ideal for materials like polymers and ceramics. They offer minimal thermal impact, making them suitable for delicate applications. On the other hand, green lasers operate at a wavelength that provides excellent absorption in metals and some polymers, enabling efficient machining and clean cuts. Each type serves distinct applications in manufacturing and technology.</p></p>
