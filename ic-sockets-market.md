<p><p>The IC sockets market encompasses various types catering to different functionalities. Production sockets are used during the manufacturing process, allowing reliable mounting and testing of integrated circuits. Test/Burn-In sockets are crucial for validating the performance and durability of ICs under stress conditions, ensuring quality before deployment. Collectively, these socket types play vital roles in semiconductor production, testing, and assembly.</p></p>
