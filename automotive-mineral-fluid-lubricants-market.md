<p><strong>The Automotive Mineral Fluid Lubricants Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paraffinic Lubricant</li><li>Naphtenic Lubricant</li><li>Aromatic Lubricant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive mineral fluid lubricants market comprises three main types: paraffinic, naphthenic, and aromatic lubricants. Paraffinic lubricants, derived from straight-chain hydrocarbons, offer excellent thermal stability and low volatility, making them popular for engine oils. Naphthenic lubricants, characterized by their cyclic hydrocarbon structure, provide superior solvency and performance in high-temperature applications. Aromatic lubricants, containing aromatic compounds, are typically used in specialized applications due to their high lubricity and ability to withstand extreme conditions, though they may have environmental concerns.</p></p>
