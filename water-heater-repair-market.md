<p><ul><li>Tank Storage</li><li>Tankless</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water heater repair market encompasses two primary types: tank storage and tankless systems. Tank storage water heaters maintain a reservoir of hot water, making them effective for high-demand situations but prone to issues like sediment buildup and heating element malfunctions. Conversely, tankless water heaters heat water on demand, offering energy efficiency and space savings; however, they may encounter problems such as flow rate limitations and ignition failures. Each type requires specialized repair approaches tailored to their operational mechanics.</p></p>
