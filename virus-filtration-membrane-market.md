<p><ul><li>Ultrafiltration Membrane</li><li>Nanofiltration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Virus Filtration Membrane Market primarily comprises ultrafiltration and nanofiltration membranes. Ultrafiltration membranes feature pore sizes typically in the range of 1-100 nm, effectively removing viruses, bacteria, and larger particles while allowing smaller molecules to pass. In contrast, nanofiltration membranes have smaller pore sizes, effectively separating larger ions and organic compounds, making them suitable for removing specific viruses and contaminants while maintaining nutrient retention. Both types play crucial roles in biopharmaceuticals, water treatment, and food processing to ensure safety.</p></p>
