<p><strong>The Dyes & Pigments Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Disperse Dyes</li><li>Reactive Dyes</li><li>Sulfur Dyes</li><li>Vat Dyes</li><li>Acid Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dyes and pigments market comprises various types tailored for specific applications. Disperse dyes are used for synthetic fibers, providing vibrant colors. Reactive dyes bond with the fabric, offering excellent wash fastness, typically in cotton. Sulfur dyes are cost-effective for darker shades and are ideal for denim. Vat dyes are known for their lightfastness and are applied to cellulose fibers. Acid dyes are primarily utilized for protein fibers and nylon, providing bright hues and good color retention.</p></p>
