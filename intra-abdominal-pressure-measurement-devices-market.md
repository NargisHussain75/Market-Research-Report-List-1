<p><p>The intra-abdominal pressure (IAP) measurement devices market encompasses two primary types: muscle-based and abdomen-based devices. Muscle-based devices are designed to assess pressure by evaluating the resistance of abdominal muscles, offering insights into muscular function and tension. On the other hand, abdomen-based devices measure direct pressure within the abdominal cavity, providing accurate readings essential for diagnosing conditions like abdominal compartment syndrome. Both device types play a critical role in clinical settings, aiding in patient monitoring and treatment planning.</p></p>
