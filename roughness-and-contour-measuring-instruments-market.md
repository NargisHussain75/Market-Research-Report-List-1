<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non-Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The roughness and contour measuring instruments market comprises two primary types: contact and non-contact instruments. Contact measuring instruments utilize physical probes that touch the surface to assess roughness and contour, providing high precision. In contrast, non-contact instruments employ optical methods, such as laser or confocal microscopy, to measure surface characteristics without physical interaction, making them suitable for delicate or reactive materials. Both types cater to various industries, ensuring accurate surface quality assessments for diverse applications.</p></p>
