<p><p>The processed cheese market is primarily segmented into two types: processed cheese and analog cheese. Processed cheese is made from a blend of natural cheese and emulsifiers, resulting in a smooth texture and extended shelf life, ideal for spreading or melting. Analog cheese, on the other hand, is a product designed to mimic cheese using non-dairy ingredients like vegetable oils and starches, catering to vegan and lactose-intolerant consumers. Both types find extensive use in food service, snacks, and convenience foods.</p></p>
