<p><p>The PET packaging market consists of two main types: rigid and flexible packaging. Rigid packaging includes containers like bottles and jars that maintain a fixed shape, providing durability and protection, commonly used for beverages and food products. Flexible packaging, on the other hand, encompasses materials such as pouches and films that are adaptable and often lighter, making them ideal for snacks, single-serve items, and other consumables. Both types leverage the advantages of PET for enhanced shelf life and recyclability.</p></p>
