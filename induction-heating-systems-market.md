<p><p>Induction heating systems are categorized into two primary types: portable and benchtop. Portable systems are compact, lightweight units designed for on-site applications, offering flexibility and ease of use for tasks such as metal hardening, soldering, or brazing in various locations. In contrast, benchtop systems are stationary, providing higher power levels and precision for industrial applications and laboratory settings. Both types leverage electromagnetic induction to rapidly heat materials, ensuring efficient energy use and consistent results across diverse operations.</p></p>
