Ceramics utilize inorganic materials to heal cracks through chemical reactions, maintaining durability and appearance. Composites combine different materials to achieve enhanced self-healing properties, often incorporating both organic and inorganic elements. Each type contributes uniquely to the advancement of protective coatings for various applications, enhancing longevity and aesthetic quality.</p></p>
