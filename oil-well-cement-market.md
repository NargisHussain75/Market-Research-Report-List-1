<p><p>The oil-well cement market comprises three main types: Ordinary, Moderate Sulfate-Resistant, and High Sulfate-Resistant cements. Ordinary cement is widely used for basic applications with minimal resistance to sulfate attacks. Moderate Sulfate-Resistant cement offers improved durability in environments with moderate sulfate levels, making it suitable for certain formations. High Sulfate-Resistant cement is designed for harsh conditions involving high sulfate concentrations, ensuring optimal performance and longevity in oil well applications, especially in challenging geological environments.</p></p>
