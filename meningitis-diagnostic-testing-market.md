<p><ul><li>Latex Agglutination Tests</li><li>PCR Assay</li><li>Lateral Flow Assay</li><li>Culture Test</li><li>ELISA Tests</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The meningitis diagnostic testing market includes various methods to identify pathogens causing meningitis. Latex agglutination tests detect antigens in bodily fluids using coated latex beads, providing rapid results. PCR assays amplify genetic material for precise pathogen identification. Lateral flow assays offer quick, user-friendly tests with visual results. Culture tests involve growing bacteria from samples, allowing for definitive diagnosis. ELISA tests measure antibodies or antigens, delivering quantitative results. These diverse methods cater to varying clinical needs and enhance disease management.</p></p>
