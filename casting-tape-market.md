<p><p>The casting tape market comprises three main types: Gypsona (Plaster of Paris), fiberglass, and polyester. Gypsona is primarily used for creating rigid casts, offering excellent moldability and positioning. Fiberglass casting tape is lightweight, waterproof, and provides durability, making it suitable for long-term use in orthopedic applications. Polyester casting tape, known for its flexibility and strength, is often used in various medical and industrial contexts. Each type serves specific needs in immobilization and support, catering to diverse user requirements.</p></p>
