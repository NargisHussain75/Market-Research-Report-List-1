<p><strong>The Vision Testing System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ffERG (Full Field Electroretinography)</li><li>mfERG (Multifocal Electroretinography)</li><li>ERG (Pattern Electroretinography)</li><li>VEP (Visual Evoked Potential)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vision testing system market encompasses various techniques for assessing retinal and visual pathway function. Full Field Electroretinography (ffERG) measures overall retinal response to light, while Multifocal Electroretinography (mfERG) evaluates localized retinal function across multiple regions. Pattern Electroretinography (ERG) assesses responses to patterned stimuli, highlighting visual processing. Visual Evoked Potential (VEP) tests the brain's response to visual stimuli, indicating the integrity of the visual pathway. Other market types may include advanced imaging technologies and diagnostic tools that complement these methods.</p></p>
