<p><p>The photovoltaic PERC cell market encompasses two main types: single-sided and double-sided PERC cells. Single-sided PERC cells feature an enhanced rear surface that reflects light, improving efficiency but only captures sunlight from one side. In contrast, double-sided PERC cells harness sunlight from both sides, significantly increasing energy output, especially in reflective environments. This dual collection capability positions double-sided PERC cells as a more advanced option, catering to diverse applications and driving growth in advanced solar technologies.</p></p>
