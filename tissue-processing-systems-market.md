<p><strong>The Tissue-Processing Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fluid Transfer Processors</li><li>Tissue Transfer Processors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The tissue-processing systems market primarily involves Fluid Transfer Processors and Tissue Transfer Processors. Fluid Transfer Processors facilitate the movement of processing solutions, ensuring efficient permeability and preservation of tissue samples. They maintain optimal flow rates and minimize contamination risks. Tissue Transfer Processors, on the other hand, handle the actual transfer of tissue specimens between various processing stations, including embedding and sectioning, ensuring consistent and high-quality sample preparation. Both types are crucial for effective tissue analysis and research in pathology.</p></p>
