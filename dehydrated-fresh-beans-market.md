<p><p>The dehydrated fresh beans market includes two main types: granules and powder. Dehydrated fresh beans granules are small, coarse particulates created by drying whole beans, retaining most nutrients and flavor. They are commonly used in soups, snacks, and seasoning blends. Dehydrated fresh beans powder, on the other hand, is finely ground and often used as a functional ingredient in baking, protein shakes, and dietary supplements. Both forms offer convenience, extended shelf life, and versatility in various culinary applications.</p></p>
