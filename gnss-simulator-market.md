<p><p>The GNSS simulator market is categorized into two main types: single-channel and multi-channel simulators. Single-channel simulators emulate one GNSS signal at a time, making them ideal for basic testing and development of GNSS receivers. In contrast, multi-channel simulators can replicate multiple signals simultaneously, allowing for more complex testing scenarios that mimic real-world conditions. This versatility is crucial for evaluating receiver performance in various environments, enhancing the robustness and reliability of GNSS technologies.</p></p>
