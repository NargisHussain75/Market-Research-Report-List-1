<p><strong>The Breast MRI Screening System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Systems</li><li>Open Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Breast MRI Screening System market is categorized into Closed Systems and Open Systems. Closed Systems feature a traditional design where the patient is enclosed in a cylindrical magnet, providing higher image resolution and improved magnetic field uniformity. In contrast, Open Systems offer a more adaptable design with a wider opening, enhancing patient comfort, especially for those with claustrophobia. While Closed Systems deliver superior imaging quality, Open Systems focus on accessibility and patient experience, influencing their respective adoption rates in screening practices.</p></p>
