<p><strong>The SiC Fibres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymer Matrix Composite(PMC)</li><li>Ceramic Matrix Composite(CMC)</li><li>Metal Matric Composite(MMC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>SiC fibers are utilized in three main composite types: Polymer Matrix Composites (PMC), Ceramic Matrix Composites (CMC), and Metal Matrix Composites (MMC). PMC incorporates SiC fibers in a polymer base, offering lightweight and flexible options for applications such as automotive and aerospace. CMC involves embedding SiC fibers in ceramic materials, enhancing thermal stability and strength, often used in high-temperature environments. MMC combines SiC fibers with metal matrices, providing superior mechanical properties and thermal conductivity, ideal for demanding industrial applications.</p></p>
