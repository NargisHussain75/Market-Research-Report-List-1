<p><p>The Artificial Pancreas Device System market includes three main types: </p><p>1. **Control-to-Range (CTR) Systems** maintain blood glucose levels within a predefined range, enhancing user safety and reducing hypoglycemia risk.</p><p>  </p><p>2. **Threshold Suspended Device Systems** automatically suspend insulin delivery when glucose levels fall below a set threshold, preventing severe hypoglycemia.</p><p>3. **Control-to-Target (CTT) Systems** aim to maintain blood glucose levels at a specific target, optimizing overall glucose management for individuals with diabetes.</p></p>
