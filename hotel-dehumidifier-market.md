<p><p>The hotel dehumidifier market comprises two main types: portable dehumidifiers and whole-home dehumidifiers. Portable dehumidifiers are compact and easily movable, allowing hotels to target specific damp areas in guest rooms or common areas, providing quick moisture control. Whole-home dehumidifiers, on the other hand, are designed to manage humidity levels throughout the entire building, offering a more integrated solution for larger spaces. Both types enhance guest comfort, preserve property integrity, and promote a healthy environment in hospitality settings.</p></p>
