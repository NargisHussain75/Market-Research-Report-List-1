<p><strong>The Shuttle Racking Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Shuttle Racking</li><li>Sub Shuttle Racking</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shuttle racking market consists of two main types: single shuttle racking and sub shuttle racking. Single shuttle racking utilizes a single autonomous shuttle to transport goods along a track within a selective racking system, optimizing storage density and retrieval speed. In contrast, sub shuttle racking employs multiple smaller shuttles that can independently access and handle goods within the same system, enhancing efficiency and flexibility. Both systems aim to maximize warehouse space and streamline inventory management processes.</p></p>
