<p><p>The automotive leaf spring suspension market primarily consists of two types: multi-leaf springs and mono-leaf springs. Multi-leaf springs are composed of multiple layers of steel strips, providing enhanced flexibility and load-bearing capacity, making them ideal for heavier vehicles. In contrast, mono-leaf springs consist of a single strip, offering a simpler and lighter design that is often used in lighter vehicles. Both types serve to absorb shocks and maintain vehicle stability, catering to different automotive applications and requirements.</p></p>
