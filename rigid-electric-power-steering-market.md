<p><strong>The Rigid Electric Power Steering Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rack Assist Type (REPS)</li><li>Colum Assist Type (CEPS)</li><li>Pinion Assist Type (PEPS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Rigid Electric Power Steering (R-EPS) market is segmented into three main types: Rack Assist Type (REPS), Column Assist Type (CEPS), and Pinion Assist Type (PEPS). REPS directly assists the rack mechanism for steering, enhancing responsiveness. CEPS offers support through the steering column, providing flexibility in design and integration. PEPS utilizes an electric motor attached to the pinion gear, delivering efficient torque assistance. Each type caters to specific vehicle requirements, optimizing performance and driver comfort.</p></p>
