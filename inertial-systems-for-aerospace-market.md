<p><p>In the aerospace market, inertial systems encompass several types: AHRS (Attitude and Heading Reference Systems) provide orientation and angular velocity data; INS (Inertial Navigation Systems) utilize accelerometers and gyroscopes for navigation without external references; IMUs (Inertial Measurement Units) combine sensors to sense motion and rotation; laser-based systems use laser technology for precise measurements of position and motion; while others include advanced technologies that enhance navigation and control capabilities in various aerospace applications. Each type serves unique roles in ensuring accurate navigation and stability.</p></p>
