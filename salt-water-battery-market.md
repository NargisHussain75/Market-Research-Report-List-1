<p><ul><li>Natural Sea Water Battery</li><li>Blended Sea Water Battery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The salt water battery market consists of two primary types: natural sea water batteries and blended sea water batteries. Natural sea water batteries utilize unprocessed seawater directly, leveraging its abundant sodium ions for energy storage, promoting eco-friendliness. In contrast, blended sea water batteries incorporate a mix of seawater and other materials, enhancing efficiency and performance while potentially optimizing energy output. Both types aim to provide sustainable alternatives for energy storage solutions, contributing to the growing demand for greener technologies.</p></p>
