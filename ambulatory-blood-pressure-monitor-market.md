<p><p>The ambulatory blood pressure monitor (ABPM) market is divided into two main types: ordinary ABPM and mobile-based ABPM. Ordinary ABPM consists of traditional devices that measure blood pressure at set intervals over 24 hours, providing comprehensive data for diagnosing hypertension. Conversely, mobile-based ABPM leverages smartphone technology, allowing users to record their blood pressure readings via mobile apps. This modern approach enhances user convenience and data accessibility, facilitating better management and monitoring of blood pressure in real-time.</p></p>
