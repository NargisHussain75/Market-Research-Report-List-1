<p><p>The hip implant market includes various types catering to different patient needs. Total replacement implants involve replacing the entire hip joint, offering comprehensive pain relief and mobility restoration. Partial replacement implants target only damaged areas, preserving healthy bone. Hip resurfacing implants cap the femoral head, preserving more bone while relieving pain and restoring function. Revision replacement implants are used to replace or repair existing implants due to failure or complications, ensuring continued mobility and comfort for patients.</p></p>
