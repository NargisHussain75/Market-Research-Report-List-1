<p><p>The hydrogen fuel cell market for boats primarily includes two types: Polymer Electrolyte Membrane Fuel Cell (PEMFC) and Solid Oxide Fuel Cell (SOFC). PEMFCs are lightweight and operate at lower temperatures, making them ideal for marine applications requiring quick start-up and high efficiency. SOFCs, on the other hand, function at elevated temperatures, offering advantages in fuel flexibility and overall efficiency for larger vessels. Both technologies enhance sustainability and reduce emissions in the maritime industry, contributing to greener boating solutions.</p></p>
