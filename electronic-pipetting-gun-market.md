<p><p>The electronic pipetting gun market is categorized into single-channel and multi-channel types. Single-channel pipettes are designed for precise, individual liquid handling, ideal for tasks requiring accuracy in small volumes. Multi-channel pipettes, on the other hand, allow simultaneous dispensing across multiple channels, enhancing efficiency in high-throughput applications such as 96-well plates. Both types cater to diverse laboratory needs, improving productivity and consistency in research and clinical environments, ultimately streamlining workflows in various scientific fields.</p></p>
