<p><ul><li>Reverse Rotation</li><li>Standard Rotation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-thermal fan clutch market features two primary types: standard rotation and reverse rotation. Standard rotation fan clutches engage in the typical direction of engine rotation, optimizing airflow and cooling for most applications. Conversely, reverse rotation fan clutches are designed for engines that rotate in the opposite direction, often found in specific vehicles such as certain marine or heavy-duty applications. Both types enhance efficiency and performance by controlling the fan's operation based on engine demands and conditions.</p></p>
