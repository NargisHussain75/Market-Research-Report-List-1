<p><p>The Automotive CO2 Heat Pump Air Conditioning market includes two main types: Direct and Indirect systems. Direct systems use CO2 as the refrigerant, directly transferring heat between the vehicle's interior and exterior. They are efficient and eco-friendly, suitable for electric vehicles. Indirect systems use a secondary fluid loop to transfer heat, with CO2 as the refrigerant in the primary loop. These systems are more complex but offer flexibility in design and integration, often used in hybrid or conventional vehicles. Both aim to reduce emissions and improve energy efficiency.</p></p>
