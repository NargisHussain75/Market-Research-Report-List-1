<p><strong>The Medical Imaging Device X-Ray Tube Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stationary anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li><li>Rotating anode Medical X-Ray Tube</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical imaging device X-ray tube market is primarily divided into two types: stationary anode and rotating anode X-ray tubes. Stationary anode tubes have a fixed target and are typically used in applications requiring lower power, making them cost-effective and simpler to operate. In contrast, rotating anode tubes possess a rotating target, allowing for higher power output and faster imaging, reducing thermal stress and improving image quality. Both types are essential for various diagnostic imaging applications in healthcare settings.</p></p>
