<p><p>The Color Selection Machine market comprises various types employing distinct technologies. Traditional optoelectronic technology coloring machines utilize light sensors for color detection and sorting. CCD technology color selection machines leverage charge-coupled devices for high-resolution imaging and precise color differentiation. Infrared technology color selection machines detect color through infrared radiation, effective in distinguishing materials based on thermal properties. X-ray technology color selection machines use X-ray imaging to identify variations in density and composition, ensuring accurate sorting of colored materials.</p></p>
