<p><strong>The Medical Nitrogen Generator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PSA</li><li>CMS</li><li>Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical nitrogen generator market comprises three primary types: Pressure Swing Adsorption (PSA), Cryogenic Air Separation (CMS), and Membrane Separators. PSA technology utilizes adsorption processes to separate nitrogen from compressed air, providing high purity. CMS involves cooling air to separate gases based on their boiling points, enabling large-scale production of liquid nitrogen. Membrane technology uses semi-permeable membranes to filter nitrogen from air, offering a compact and energy-efficient solution for hospitals. Each type serves specific requirements in medical applications.</p></p>
