<p><strong>The Stream Gauge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Stream Gauge</li><li>Ultrasonic Stream Gauge</li><li>Turbine Stream Gauge</li><li>Magnetic Stream Gauge</li><li>Coriolis</li><li>Vortex</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Stream gauges are vital for measuring fluid flow, with various types suited to different applications. Positive displacement stream gauges capture fluid volume directly, while ultrasonic stream gauges use sound waves for non-intrusive flow measurement. Turbine stream gauges utilize a rotating impeller for accuracy, whereas magnetic stream gauges detect flow based on magnetic fields. Coriolis gauges measure mass flow through fluid-induced vibrations, and vortex gauges rely on the swirling motion of fluid to determine flow rate. Each type is crucial for specific industries and applications.</p></p>
