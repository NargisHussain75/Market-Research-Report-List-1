<p><ul><li>Deep Groove Ball Bearings</li><li>Angular Contact Ball Bearings</li><li>Self-Aligning Ball Bearings</li><li>Thrust Ball Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ball bearing market comprises several key types, each serving distinct applications. Deep groove ball bearings are versatile, accommodating radial and axial loads in various machinery. Angular contact ball bearings are designed for high-speed applications, supporting axial loads in one direction. Self-aligning ball bearings feature a unique design that allows for misalignment, ideal for applications with shaft deflection. Thrust ball bearings are used for axial loads, providing stability in gear and bearing systems, essential for efficient machinery operation.</p></p>
