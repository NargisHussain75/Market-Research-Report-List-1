<p><ul><li>Uncooled Type</li><li>Cooled Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Thermal imaging technology is classified into two main types: uncooled and cooled thermal imaging systems. Uncooled thermal cameras operate at ambient temperatures, utilizing materials that change resistance with heat, making them compact and cost-effective for applications such as surveillance and automotive safety. In contrast, cooled thermal systems require a cryogenic cooling mechanism to reduce sensor temperatures, enabling higher sensitivity and resolution, making them ideal for military, aerospace, and industrial applications. Both types cater to diverse market needs based on performance and application.</p></p>
