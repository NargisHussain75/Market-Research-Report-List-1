<p><strong>The Pad Printing Ink for Medical Products Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent-based</li><li>UV Curing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pad printing ink for medical products is categorized primarily into solvent-based and UV curing types. Solvent-based inks utilize volatile solvents for adhesion and are favored for their versatility and vibrant colors. However, they may raise concerns about VOC emissions. In contrast, UV curing inks involve a photoinitiated polymerization process that offers rapid drying and robust adhesion, making them suitable for non-porous surfaces. Both types ensure compliance with stringent medical standards, catering to diverse product needs in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
