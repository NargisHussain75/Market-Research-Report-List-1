<p><p>Semi-automatic carton sealers are categorized based on their driving mechanisms: side belt-driven, top and bottom belt-driven, and others. Side belt-driven sealers utilize belts on the sides to secure cartons, providing stability during sealing. Top and bottom belt-driven models apply pressure from both sides for enhanced sealing efficiency. Other types may include various configurations like adjustable heights and hybrid systems to accommodate diverse carton sizes and operational needs, optimizing packaging processes across industries.</p></p>
