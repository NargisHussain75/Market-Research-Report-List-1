<p><p>Aramid Fiber Reinforced Polymer (AFRP) is a composite material combining aramid fibers with a polymer matrix, widely used for its high strength-to-weight ratio and thermal stability. The market for AFRP is divided into two main types: thermoplastic and thermoset. Thermoplastic AFRP offers advantages like recyclability and ease of processing, making it suitable for applications requiring impact resistance. In contrast, thermoset AFRP provides superior mechanical properties and durability, ideal for aerospace and automotive industries where performance and stability under stress are crucial.</p></p>
