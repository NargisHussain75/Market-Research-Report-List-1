<p><ul><li>Polycrystalline Diamond Powder</li><li>Monocrystalline Diamond Powder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Micron Diamond Powder market comprises two primary types: polycrystalline diamond powder and monocrystalline diamond powder. Polycrystalline diamond powder is formed from numerous diamond grains, offering high toughness and thermal stability, making it ideal for grinding and cutting applications. In contrast, monocrystalline diamond powder, consisting of single crystal structures, provides superior hardness and precision, suitable for high-performance tools and precision machining. Both types cater to diverse industries, including manufacturing, electronics, and jewelry, based on their specific properties and applications.</p></p>
