<p><ul><li>HDPE</li><li>LDPE</li><li>MDPE</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PE bottles market is categorized into three main types: HDPE, LDPE, and MDPE. HDPE (High-Density Polyethylene) offers strength and rigidity, making it ideal for containers and chemical storage. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) is flexible and resistant to impact, often used for squeeze bottles and films. MDPE (Medium-Density Polyethylene) strikes a balance between the two, providing moderate strength and flexibility, suitable for moderate-use applications. Each type caters to different packaging needs based on durability and flexibility requirements.</p></p>
