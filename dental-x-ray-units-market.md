<p><p>The dental X-Ray units market primarily consists of two types: intraoral and extraoral X-ray units. Intraoral X-ray units are designed for imaging within the mouth, allowing dentists to capture detailed images of teeth and surrounding tissues. Extraoral X-ray units, on the other hand, are used to obtain images from outside the mouth, useful for capturing broader views of the jaw and skull, aiding in treatment planning and diagnosis. Both types are essential for comprehensive dental care.</p></p>
