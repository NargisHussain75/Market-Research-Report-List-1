<p><p>The baby formula market utilizes palm oil primarily in two forms: crude palm oil and palm olein. Crude palm oil is extracted directly from the fruit and is rich in nutrients, providing essential fatty acids, vitamins A and E. It undergoes minimal processing and retains many natural properties. Palm olein, a refined version of crude palm oil, has a lower melting point and is commonly used for its stability and texture in formulations. Both types are valued for their cost-effectiveness and nutritional benefits.</p></p>
