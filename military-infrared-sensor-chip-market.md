<p><p>The Military Infrared Sensor Chip market comprises two main types: refrigeration type and uncooled type. Refrigeration type sensors utilize cryogenic cooling to enhance sensitivity and performance, making them ideal for high-precision applications in challenging environments. In contrast, uncooled type sensors operate at ambient temperatures, offering advantages like lower power consumption, reduced size, and cost-effectiveness, while still delivering adequate performance for many military applications. Both types play crucial roles in surveillance, target tracking, and situational awareness in defense systems.</p></p>
