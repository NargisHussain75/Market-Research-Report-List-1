<p><p>The Reusable Transesophageal Echocardiography Probe Disinfectors market consists of two primary disinfection types: Intermediate/Low-Level Disinfection (LLDs/ILDs) and High-Level Disinfection (HLD). LLDs/ILDs are designed to reduce microbial presence but may not eliminate all spores, making them suitable for non-critical surfaces. In contrast, HLD systems ensure the complete eradication of most pathogens, including spores, and are essential for semi-critical medical devices. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining patient safety and infection control in healthcare settings.</p></p>
