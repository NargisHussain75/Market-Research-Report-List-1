<p><p>The connected vehicle device market comprises two main types: built-in devices and retrofit devices. Built-in devices are integrated directly into the vehicle during manufacturing, offering seamless connectivity and performance. These systems often include advanced features such as navigation, telematics, and safety enhancements. In contrast, retrofit devices are aftermarket solutions that can be installed in existing vehicles to provide connectivity and functionality. They cater to consumers seeking to upgrade their older models with modern technology, enabling features like real-time monitoring and remote access.</p></p>
