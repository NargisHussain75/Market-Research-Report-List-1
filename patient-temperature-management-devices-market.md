<p><ul><li>Patient Warming Systems</li><li>Patient Cooling Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Patient Temperature Management Devices market is segmented into two main types: patient warming systems and patient cooling systems. Patient warming systems are designed to prevent hypothermia during surgical procedures and critical care by maintaining optimal body temperature. These systems often utilize heated blankets, wraps, and fluid warmers. Conversely, patient cooling systems are employed to reduce body temperature in situations like heatstroke or post-surgery recovery, using cooling blankets and ice packs to enhance patient comfort and improve outcomes.</p></p>
