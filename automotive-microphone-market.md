<p><strong>The Automotive Microphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>ECM Microphone</li><li>MEMS Microphone</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive Microphone Market includes ECM (Electret Condenser Microphone) and MEMS (Micro-Electro-Mechanical Systems) microphones. ECM microphones are traditional, cost-effective, and widely used for basic voice capture in vehicles. They rely on electret material for sound detection but are bulkier and less durable. MEMS microphones, on the other hand, are compact, robust, and offer superior noise cancellation and performance in harsh environments. They are increasingly preferred for advanced automotive applications like voice assistants and in-car communication systems due to their miniaturization and integration capabilities.</p></p>
