<p><ul><li>Hollow Microneedle Technology</li><li>Solid Microneedle Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The microneedles market is divided into two main types: hollow microneedle technology and solid microneedle technology. Hollow microneedles are designed to deliver liquids, such as vaccines and medications, directly into the skin, enhancing absorption and efficacy. In contrast, solid microneedles are employed primarily for drug delivery through passive diffusion or as a mechanism for solid or powdered formulations. Both technologies offer advantages in pain reduction, improved patient compliance, and the potential for self-administration in various therapeutic applications.</p></p>
