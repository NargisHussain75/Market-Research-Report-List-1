<p><p>The veterinary medication market can be categorized into three primary types: oral, injection, and other methods. Oral medications include tablets, capsules, and liquids administered via the mouth, offering ease of administration for pet owners. Injection medications are delivered directly into the bloodstream or muscle, ensuring rapid absorption and effectiveness, commonly used for vaccines and critical care. Other methods encompass topical treatments, inhalants, and transdermal patches, providing diverse options for specific conditions and species, enhancing overall animal health management.</p></p>
