<p><p>The P-Type battery market includes various technologies, primarily focusing on PERC (Passivated Emitter and Rear Cell) and BSF (Bifacial Solar Cell) batteries. PERC batteries enhance efficiency by passivating the rear surface, reducing electron recombination, while BSF batteries utilize bifacial designs to capture sunlight on both sides, boosting energy output. Both technologies aim to improve solar cell performance, driving advancements in renewable energy. Their growing adoption reflects a trend towards more efficient and cost-effective solar energy solutions in the market.</p></p>
