Differential pressure sensors assess the pressure drop across filters, indicating clogging. Temperature sensors track exhaust temperatures to prevent overheating. Other sensors may include hydrocarbon or ammonia sensors, contributing to comprehensive emissions management and compliance with environmental regulations.</p></p>
