<p><p>Cationic fatliquor is a type of chemical agent used in the leather tanning process to enhance the softness, flexibility, and durability of leather products. This results in improved water resistance, comfort, and overall leather quality. </p><p>The cationic fatliquor market is witnessing significant growth due to rising demand for high-quality leather in various applications, including footwear, automotive upholstery, and fashion accessories.
