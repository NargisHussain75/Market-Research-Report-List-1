<p><strong>The Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) Contrast Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Paramagnetic Contrast Agent</li><li>Superparamagnetic Contrast Agents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) contrast agents enhance the quality of images by altering the magnetic properties of nearby tissues. Paramagnetic contrast agents, primarily gadolinium-based, enhance signal intensity by shortening relaxation times of protons, making lesions more visible. Superparamagnetic contrast agents, often composed of iron oxide nanoparticles, provide increased magnetic susceptibility, improving image contrast by creating local magnetic field disturbances. Both types are critical for accurate diagnostics in various medical applications, aiding in disease detection and characterization.</p></p>
