<p><ul><li>SDI</li><li>TDI</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The common-rail injection EFI (Electronic Fuel Injection) engine market features variations like SDI (Suction Diesel Injection) and TDI (Turbocharged Direct Injection). SDI utilizes a simpler, atmospheric pressure system for fuel delivery, enhancing reliability but providing lower power output. In contrast, TDI employs turbocharging along with direct fuel injection, resulting in improved efficiency and higher performance. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with TDI being favored for its power and performance, while SDI appeals to those prioritizing durability and simplicity.</p></p>
