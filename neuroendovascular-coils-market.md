<p><p>The neuroendovascular coils market comprises two primary types: bare platinum coils and coated bioactive coils. Bare platinum coils are used to occlude blood vessels in aneurysms, offering radiopacity and reliability in neurovascular interventions. Coated bioactive coils, on the other hand, are designed with surfaces that promote healing and reduce the risk of aneurysm recurrence by enhancing the biological response. These innovations aim to improve patient outcomes by balancing mechanical stability and biological integration in vascular treatments.</p></p>
