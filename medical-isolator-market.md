<p><p>The medical isolator market is primarily divided into two types: floor-standing and mobile types. Floor-standing isolators are stationary units designed for permanent placement in laboratories or clean rooms, providing a controlled environment for handling hazardous materials. In contrast, mobile isolators are portable units that can be easily moved between various locations, offering flexibility for different applications. Both types ensure safety and contamination prevention, catering to the needs of healthcare, pharmaceutical, and research sectors in managing biohazardous substances effectively.</p></p>
