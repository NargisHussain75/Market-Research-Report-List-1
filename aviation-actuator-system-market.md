<p><strong>The Aviation Actuator System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromechanical</li><li>Electro Hydraulic</li><li>Electro Hydrostatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aviation actuator system market consists of three primary types: electromechanical, electro-hydraulic, and electro-hydrostatic actuators. Electromechanical actuators convert electrical energy into mechanical motion, often used for simpler applications. Electro-hydraulic actuators combine hydraulic systems with electrical control, providing high force and precise movement, suitable for larger aircraft systems. Electro-hydrostatic actuators integrate hydraulic and electrical components in a compact design, offering efficiency and reliability for advanced aerospace applications, particularly in flight control surfaces and landing gear systems.</p></p>
