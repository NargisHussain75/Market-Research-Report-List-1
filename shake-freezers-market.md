<p><ul><li>Water Cooling</li><li>Air Cooling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Shake freezers are classified into two main market types: water cooling and air cooling. Water cooling shake freezers use a liquid refrigerant circulated through coils to dissipate heat, ensuring efficient cooling and operation, particularly in high-demand environments. In contrast, air cooling shake freezers rely on air circulation and fans to regulate temperature. While water cooling typically offers faster cooling times, air cooling systems are often more energy-efficient and easier to maintain, making both options suitable for various commercial applications.</p></p>
