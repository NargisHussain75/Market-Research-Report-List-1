<p><ul><li>Mass Spectrometry</li><li>Atomic Spectrometry</li><li>Molecular Spectrometry</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The laboratory spectrometer market is divided into three primary types: mass spectrometry, atomic spectrometry, and molecular spectrometry. Mass spectrometry analyzes the mass-to-charge ratio of ions, making it essential for identifying chemical compounds and their concentrations. Atomic spectrometry focuses on the elemental composition of samples through techniques like atomic absorption and emission. Molecular spectrometry examines the interaction of molecules with electromagnetic radiation to determine molecular structures and concentrations. These technologies play crucial roles in research, pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and quality control.</p></p>
