<p><p>The polyurethane insulation board market is dominated by two main types: Expanded Polystyrene Foam (EPS) and Extruded Polystyrene Foam (XPS). EPS is lightweight, moisture-resistant, and cost-effective, making it suitable for various applications, including walls and roofs. XPS, on the other hand, offers superior compressive strength and lower thermal conductivity, ideal for demanding environments like below-grade insulation. Both types are essential for enhancing energy efficiency in construction, contributing to sustainable building practices.</p></p>
