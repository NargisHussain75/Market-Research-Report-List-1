<p><ul><li>Retrograde Stents</li><li>Antegrade Stents</li><li>Internal (double-J) Stents</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The radioactive stent market encompasses various types tailored for specific medical conditions. Retrograde stents are designed for placing via the bladder, aiding in urinary tract obstructions. Antegrade stents, on the other hand, are inserted through the kidneys to manage similar blockages. Internal (double-J) stents facilitate urine flow from the kidney to the bladder, ensuring drainage while minimizing tissue irritation. Each stent type serves a unique purpose, enhancing patient outcomes in the management of urological conditions.</p></p>
