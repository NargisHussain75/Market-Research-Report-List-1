<p><p>The inorganic adhesives market comprises several types, each with distinct chemical properties and applications. Silicate type adhesives are known for their strong binding capabilities and are often used in construction. Sulfate type adhesives offer excellent resistance to moisture and heat, making them ideal for industrial applications. Phosphate type adhesives provide high-temperature stability and are commonly used in ceramics. Borate type adhesives possess anti-fungal properties, suitable for wood and other organic materials. Other market types include various specialized formulations tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
