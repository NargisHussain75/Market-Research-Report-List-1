<p><p>The non-destructive testing (NDT) equipment market is diverse, encompassing several key methods. Magnetic Particle Testing identifies surface and near-surface defects by using magnetic fields and fine particles. Visual Testing relies on direct observation to detect flaws. Liquid Penetrant Testing reveals surface-breaking defects through color contrast agents. Radiographic Testing employs X-rays or gamma rays to visualize internal structures. Ultrasonic Testing uses high-frequency sound waves to assess material integrity. Other methods include eddy current and acoustic emission testing, catering to specific industry needs.</p></p>
