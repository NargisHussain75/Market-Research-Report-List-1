<p><p>The EDM wire market comprises three main types: No Coated Wire, Coated Wire, and Hybrid Wire. No Coated Wire is uncoated and ideal for specific applications requiring high precision. Coated Wire features a protective layer enhancing its performance and durability, making it suitable for a broader range of applications. Hybrid Wire combines characteristics of both, offering benefits like improved conductivity and reduced wear, thereby optimizing cutting efficiency. Each type caters to different industrial needs in electrical discharge machining processes.</p></p>
