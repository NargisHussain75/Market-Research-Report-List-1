<p><strong>The Alcohol free Beer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fermentation-limited Method</li><li>Dealcoholization Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The alcohol-free beer market comprises two main types based on production methods: fermentation-limited and dealcoholization. The fermentation-limited method restricts the fermentation process to yield minimal alcohol, resulting in a naturally low-alcohol beer. In contrast, the dealcoholization method involves brewing regular beer and then removing alcohol through various techniques, such as reverse osmosis or vacuum distillation. Each method offers distinct flavor profiles and consumer preferences, catering to the growing demand for non-alcoholic beverage options.</p></p>
