<p><p>Amphibious Combat Vehicles (ACVs) are designed for both land and water operations, featuring various propulsion systems. Screw propeller ACVs use rotating blades for efficient water movement, ideal for calm waters. Water jet ACVs propel water through a jet system, offering higher speeds and maneuverability in rough conditions. Track-based ACVs rely on tracks for land mobility, providing stability and traction on uneven terrain. Other types include hybrid systems or advanced technologies, catering to diverse operational needs, balancing speed, agility, and terrain adaptability for military and defense applications.</p></p>
