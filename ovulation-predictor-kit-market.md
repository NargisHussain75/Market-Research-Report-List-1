<p><p>The Ovulation Predictor Kit market is primarily divided into urine-based and saliva-based testing methods. Urine-based kits detect hormonal changes in urine, identifying the surge in luteinizing hormone that signals ovulation, making them popular for their accuracy and ease of use. Saliva-based kits analyze hormonal fluctuations through saliva samples, using microscopic patterns of dried saliva to pinpoint ovulation. While urine tests are more widely adopted, saliva tests appeal to those seeking a non-invasive and reusable option, enhancing user convenience.</p></p>
