<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Corrugated Carton Ink Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water-Based Inks</li><li>Solvent-Based Inks</li><li>UV-Cured Inks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The corrugated carton ink market comprises three main types: water-based inks, solvent-based inks, and UV-cured inks. Water-based inks are eco-friendly, utilizing water as a solvent, making them low in volatile organic compounds (VOCs). Solvent-based inks are durable and offer vibrant colors but generally contain higher VOC levels, posing environmental concerns. UV-cured inks use ultraviolet light to quickly cure, providing excellent adhesion and gloss, and are ideal for high-speed applications. Each type caters to specific printing needs and sustainability considerations.</p></p>
