<p><strong>The Soft-Rock Shiled Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slurry Shield (SS)</li><li>Earth Pressure Balance Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Soft-Rock Shield Machine market includes various types designed for underground excavation, primarily focusing on Slurry Shield (SS) and Earth Pressure Balance (EPB) machines. Slurry Shield machines utilize a slurry mixture to stabilize the tunnel face and remove excavated material, making them ideal for soft and wet ground conditions. In contrast, Earth Pressure Balance machines maintain ground stability by balancing the pressure during excavation, effectively managing both soft and cohesive soils. These innovations enhance safety and efficiency in tunneling projects.</p></p>
