<p><p>The handheld cable fault locators market is segmented into several types based on the nature of faults identified. Short-circuit locators detect faults caused by unintended connections between conductors, while earth fault locators identify issues where current leaks to the ground, indicating insulation failures. Cable breaks refer to complete interruptions in the cable line, necessitating precise identification for repairs. Other fault types encompass varied defects like intermittent faults and high-resistance faults, broadening the applicability of these specialized diagnostic tools.</p></p>
