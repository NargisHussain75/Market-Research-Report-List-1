<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic</li><li>Infrared</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Dairy Analyzer market is primarily divided into two types: ultrasonic and infrared. Ultrasonic analyzers utilize sound waves to measure the properties of milk, providing accurate readings of fat content, protein levels, and other components through density changes. In contrast, infrared analyzers employ light waves to assess the chemical composition of dairy products, offering rapid and efficient analysis. Both technologies enhance quality control in dairy production, ensuring compliance with standards and improving product consistency and safety.</p></p>
