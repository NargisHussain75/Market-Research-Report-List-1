<p><p>Laminar airflow cabinets are vital in laboratory environments for protecting samples and personnel from contamination. Vertical laminar airflow cabinets direct airflow vertically, creating a sterile work area suitable for handling sensitive materials. In contrast, horizontal laminar airflow cabinets provide airflow from the rear towards the front, ensuring a contaminant-free zone ideal for applications such as microbiology and pharmaceutical research. Both types are designed to maintain clean conditions, but the choice depends on the specific requirements of the laboratory workflow.</p></p>
