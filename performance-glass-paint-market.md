Pyrolytic coating involves the application of a thin film during glass manufacturing, offering durability and UV protection. Sputtered coating utilizes a vacuum process to deposit metal or ceramic layers, improving reflectivity and aesthetics. Screen coating applies patterns or colors through screen printing, while spray coating offers versatility in application and finish. Other methods may include ceramic or dip coatings, catering to diverse performance and design needs in various industries.</p></p>
