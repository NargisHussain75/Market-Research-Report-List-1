Powder is utilized for additive manufacturing techniques, enabling the creation of customized medical solutions through 3D printing. Bars are typically selected for machining applications, providing solid structural components for orthopedic implants and surgical instruments. Each form offers distinct advantages tailored to specific medical applications, ensuring high performance and biocompatibility.</p></p>
