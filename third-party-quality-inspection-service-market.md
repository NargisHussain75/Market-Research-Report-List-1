<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Third Party Quality Inspection Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-Production Inspection (PPI)</li><li>During Production Inspection (DPI)</li><li>Pre-shipment Inspection (PSI)</li><li>Loading Supervision (LS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Third Party Quality Inspection Service market consists of four key types. Pre-Production Inspection (PPI) evaluates materials and processes before manufacturing begins, ensuring compliance with standards. During Production Inspection (DPI) monitors quality and progress during the production phase, identifying defects early. Pre-shipment Inspection (PSI) verifies product quality and quantity before shipment, preventing issues post-delivery. Loading Supervision (LS) involves overseeing the loading process to ensure products are handled properly and securely, mitigating damage risks during transport.</p></p>
